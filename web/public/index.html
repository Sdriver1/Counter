Everything you need

Built for communities that take their counting channel seriously.

Math Expressions

Type sqrt(144), 2^8, or (5*4)+2. Real math is accepted and validated before it counts.

Six Counting Modes

Normal, Fibonacci, Prime, Even, Odd, and Perfect Squares — all managed by a single bot.

Anti-Cheat

No double counting. Blacklist bad actors, whitelist trusted users, and reset on any violation.

Streak Tracking

Counts consecutive correct answers per channel. Break a streak of 10+ and the whole server feels it.

Leaderboards

Paginated per-server leaderboards show who contributes the most to your counting channels.

Admin Controls

Switch modes on the fly, set slowmode, manage access lists, and reset — all via slash commands.

Pick your challenge

Six unique modes from casual to brutal.

Normal

1 → 2 → 3 → 4 → 5 → 6

The classic. Simple sequential counting for any server.

Even

2 → 4 → 6 → 8 → 10 → 12

Even numbers only. Steady and beginner-friendly.

Odd

1 → 3 → 5 → 7 → 9 → 11

Odd numbers only. The road less taken.

Fibonacci

Hard
1 → 1 → 2 → 3 → 5 → 8 → 13

Each number is the sum of the previous two. Gets painful fast.

Prime

Hard
2 → 3 → 5 → 7 → 11 → 13

Only prime numbers. No composites, no mercy.

Squares

Hard
1 → 4 → 9 → 16 → 25 → 36

Perfect squares only. Looks easy on paper.

Commands

Everything you need to run your counting channel.

/setup-counter Admin

Set up a counting channel with your chosen mode. Supports all six modes via autocomplete.

/remove-counter Admin

Remove a counting channel and all associated configuration from your server.

/counter-setting Admin

Switch mode, set slowmode, manage blacklist / whitelist, and manually reset the count.

/stats Everyone

View server counting stats, streaks, and a paginated leaderboard of top counters.

/botstats Everyone

Global bot stats — guilds, total users, total counts, and record highs per mode.

/reset-counter Admin

Manually reset the active counter to zero without changing any other settings.
